About Me

Study

I studied at Trinity College Dublin to a Masters' Degree level. My degree was in Mechanical and Manufacturing Engineering. In this course I enhanced my problem solving skills and developed a strong work ethic. My main drive at this point was physics and maths but I gained some exposure to programming at this early stage, working with C++ for my computer modules.

For my Masters thesis, I was assessing the effectiveness of using a crash test software called MADYMO to help diagnose sports concussion injuries. My passion for software/IT started to grow during this time

Work

After university, I started work at global semiconductor manufacturer Infineon Technologies where I work today. My role is as a supply chain Order Managment Specialist. I work alot on process engineering; optimizing our order management processes in SAP to be as efficient as possible.

I also coordinate RPA projects to help automate our operations as much as possible. There is a big need for automating manual tasks within my team; this is where I have worked extensively with VBA to automate various spreadsheet tasks such as report generation, database maintenance, email sending and much more.

Now

Having become fluent at using VBA and enjoyed programming so much, I decided I wanted to pursue a career in development. I enrolled in Code Insititute's Full Stack Software Development Diploma and am currently doing the course part time.

I am learning various front end and back end languages such as HTML, CSS, Javascript and Python. I am also advancing my skills as much as I can in my spare time and building up my github portfolio with projects I have developed in VBA.

Currently, I am working on my first Code Institute milestone project. You can find details of it and much more by jumping to Projects
